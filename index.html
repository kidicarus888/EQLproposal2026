Background & Context

The Challenge

Chronic diseases ‚Äî specifically diabetes, prediabetes, and metabolic disease risk (e.g., hypertension, obesity) ‚Äî remain among the top drivers of corporate healthcare costs in Indonesia.

The Solution

Early intervention through continuous lifestyle management is proven to improve employee health, productivity, and cost efficiency. Loop Health provides measurable, scalable programs that combine education, data tracking, and human coaching to drive this change.

The Evidence

Research consistently shows that modest weight loss (5-7%) can significantly reduce the risk of developing type 2 diabetes and improve conditions like hypertension. Our programs are built on this proven principle.

*Reference: The Diabetes Prevention Program (DPP) Research Group. (2002). Reduction in the incidence of type 2 diabetes with lifestyle intervention or metformin. N Engl J Med, 346(8), 393-403.

Program Features: Management & Prevention

Program 1: Diabetes Management (DMP)

A 12-month structured program for employees already living with diabetes.

  • Personalized digital health coaching (7am‚Äì10pm daily)
  • Regular tracking of blood glucose, food, and activities
  • Monthly video consultations with Loop coaches
  • Structured educational content and reminders
  • OKI Pilot Outcome: 78% improved blood glucose

Program 2: Digital Prevention (DPP)

A 6-month lifestyle program for employees who are overweight or at risk.

  • Digital risk screening (BMI, waist, glucose risk)
  • Personalized habit coaching and goal tracking
  • Weight and activity monitoring
  • Monthly virtual consultations and progress review
  • Early Data Outcome: 3.3% avg. weight reduction in 2 months

Key Parameters for Measurement

Health Parameters (Clinical Outcomes)

  • HbA1c (Baseline, 6mo, 12mo) - DMP Focus
